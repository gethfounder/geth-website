What is GETH?

GETH (Global Energy Trade Hub) is an idea-stage project designed to become a digital ecosystem for over-the-counter (OTC) commodity trading. We aim to address critical pain points in the $4.6 trillion market: manual processes, certificate fraud, and dispute resolution delays.

Our approach: secure the legal foundation first (UAE VARA + DMCC licensing), then build an automated platform with AI-driven counterparty scoring, smart contract-based escrow, and real-time cargo tracking — and finally validate with test deals from the founder's industry network.

Legal Framework & Compliance

Enterprise-grade legal infrastructure for institutional commodity trading

GETH operates as a technology platform — not as a trading house, carrier, or insurer. We provide the digital infrastructure that connects verified counterparties, regulated banks, licensed inspection companies, and professional intermediaries within a compliant ecosystem.

⚖️

Platform Legal Status

GETH is a digital intermediary — we do not buy, sell, transport, or insure commodities. All commercial transactions occur directly between verified counterparties.

  • Platform operator under UAE Electronic Transactions Law
  • Technology infrastructure provider
  • No proprietary trading activities
  • No custody of physical assets
  • Regulated via VARA + DMCC licensing
  • English Common Law governance (DIFC)
🚢

Logistics Legal Framework

GETH integrates with logistics providers through standardized legal agreements, ensuring clear allocation of responsibilities and risks.

  • Master Service Agreement (MSA) with shipping companies
  • API Integration Agreement for booking & tracking
  • Electronic Bill of Lading (eBL) — legally recognized
  • Incoterms® 2020 guidance via AI assistant
  • Customs documentation automation
  • Force Majeure & dispute clauses
🛡️

Insurance Legal Framework

GETH acts as insurance aggregator & facilitator — policies are issued by licensed insurers through API integration.

  • Insurance Brokerage Agreement with Lloyd's Coverholders
  • Smart Insurance Policy with automated triggers
  • Institute Cargo Clauses (A/B/C) standard coverage
  • NFT Insurance Certificate as proof of coverage
  • Automated claims processing via smart contracts
  • Blockchain evidence for dispute resolution
📜

Smart Contracts as Legal Instruments

Smart contracts on GETH are legally binding agreements under UAE and international law, with immutable audit trails.

  • Recognized under UAE Federal Law No. 1 of 2022
  • ADGM Smart Transaction Regulations 2021
  • DIFC Digital Assets Law 2023
  • UNCITRAL Model Law compliance
  • Blockchain evidence admissible in courts
  • Digital signatures with Qualified Electronic Signature

Compliance & Regulatory

Planned Step-by-Step Process

1

📤 Lot Creation & Verification

Seller uploads 50,000 MT fuel oil specification to GETH platform, attaches SGS quality certificate. AI system verifies seller's credentials, financial standing, and trading history.

Target: Counterparty risk scored in <10 minutes
2

🔍 AI Matching & Discovery

GETH's AI engine analyses buyer requirements and automatically matches with available lots. If an intermediary is involved, they receive a notification with a pre-calculated commission structure. Alternatively, the buyer and seller connect directly.

Target: Automated deal discovery — no cold calls
3

🔒 Escrow & Contract Lock

Buyer books the lot and deposits $19M into regulated escrow account. Smart contract locks all terms: price, quality specs, delivery timeline, intermediary commission (if applicable).

Target: 1.10% all-in fee ($209,000)
4

🚢 Logistics Coordination

Seller confirms cargo readiness. Buyer selects preferred shipping route. Intermediary or buyer coordinates with carrier for vessel booking. IoT sensors activated for real-time tracking.

Target: Transparent logistics for all parties
5

📡 Real-Time Cargo Tracking

IoT devices monitor cargo temperature, humidity, and location throughout voyage. Satellite data streamed to GETH platform. Automated notifications at key milestones.

Target: Tamper-proof tracking data
6

✅ Quality Verification

Upon arrival at Mumbai port, an accredited independent inspector from SGS or Bureau Veritas conducts physical cargo inspection against the NFT certificate specifications. Inspection results are transmitted via secure API directly to the GETH platform. The smart contract automatically cross-verifies API data against original certificate terms. If quality matches specifications and no disputes are flagged, the contract proceeds to settlement.

Target: Instant confirmation — no 18-month disputes
7

💰 Automated Settlement

Smart contract triggers automated settlement and distributes payments via bank webhooks: $18,753,000 to seller (after 1.10% GETH platform fee of $209,000), and $38,000 to the intermediary (if involved). The intermediary is typically a commodity brokerage firm that originated the deal, providing deal origination, counterparty vetting, and logistics coordination. If the buyer and seller found each other directly through GETH's AI matching, this commission is saved, maximizing value for both parties. Settlement completes in hours, not weeks.

Target: $209,000 GETH revenue (1.10% of deal)
8

📊 Reporting & Analytics

All participants access deal history via dashboard. Buyer receives comprehensive digital passport with ESG data. Complete audit trail for compliance.

Target: Complete audit trail for compliance
