What is GETH?

GETH (Global Energy & Commodity Trust Hub) is a next-generation platform designed to digitise and streamline over-the-counter (OTC) commodity trading. We address critical pain points in the $4.6 trillion market: 63% manual processes, widespread certificate fraud costing $50B+ annually, and dispute resolution delays exceeding 18 months.

Our phased approach starts with a concierge MVP — manually orchestrating deals with AI-assisted counterparty scoring and stablecoin payments — then evolves into a fully automated ecosystem with smart contracts, NFT-based quality certificates, IoT cargo tracking, and a dual-token economy ($GETH + $vGETH).

Step-by-Step Process

1

📤 Lot Creation & Verification

Seller uploads 50,000 MT fuel oil specification to GETH platform, attaches SGS quality certificate (minted as NFT in Phase 2). AI system instantly verifies seller's credentials, financial standing, and trading history.

✓ Counterparty risk scored in <10 minutes
2

🔍 AI Matching & Discovery

GETH's AI engine analyses buyer requirements (API gravity, sulphur content, delivery window) and automatically matches with available lots. Intermediary receives notification about potential deal with pre-calculated commission structure ($38,000 total).

✓ Automated deal discovery — no cold calls
3

🔒 Escrow & Contract Lock

Buyer books the lot and deposits $19M into regulated escrow account (RAKBANK or equivalent). Smart contract (Phase 2) or legal agreement (Phase 1) locks all terms: price, quality specs, delivery timeline, intermediary commission split ($19k upfront + $19k on completion).

✓ 0.5% platform fee + 0.1% escrow fee applied
4

🚢 Logistics Coordination

Seller confirms cargo readiness at Mina-Said terminal. Buyer selects preferred shipping route via GETH interface. Intermediary coordinates with Maersk or other carrier for vessel booking. IoT sensors activated for real-time tracking.

✓ Transparent logistics — all parties see same data
5

📡 Real-Time Cargo Tracking

IoT devices monitor cargo temperature, humidity, and location throughout 45-day voyage. Satellite data streamed to GETH platform. All participants receive automated notifications at key milestones (departure, Suez Canal transit, arrival).

✓ Tamper-proof tracking data — no disputes about conditions
6

✅ Quality Verification & Acceptance

Upon arrival at Mumbai port, independent inspector (SGS/Bureau Veritas) verifies cargo quality against NFT certificate specs. Buyer digitally signs acceptance. IoT data confirms no temperature excursions or contamination during transit.

✓ Instant quality confirmation — no 18-month disputes
7

💰 Automated Settlement

Smart contract (Phase 2) or escrow agent (Phase 1) releases $18.96M to seller (minus $5k platform fee), $38k to intermediary (split: $19k + $19k), and issues digital ESG passport to buyer. Entire settlement completes in <2 hours.

✓ Total GETH revenue: $28,500 (0.15% of deal value)
8

📊 Reporting & Analytics

All participants access deal history via GETH dashboard. Seller sees completed transaction in portfolio. Buyer receives comprehensive digital passport with ESG data (carbon footprint, sustainability metrics). Intermediary generates client report via analytics hub.

✓ Complete audit trail — ready for compliance & tax
