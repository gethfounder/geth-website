What is GETH?

GETH (Global Energy Trade Hub) is an idea-stage project designed to become a next-generation automated digital ecosystem for over-the-counter (OTC) commodity trading. We aim to address critical pain points in the $4.6 trillion market: 63% manual processes, certificate fraud costing $50B+ annually, and dispute resolution delays exceeding 18 months.

Our approach: secure the legal foundation first (UAE VARA + DMCC licensing), then build the automated platform with AI-driven counterparty scoring, smart contract-based escrow, and real-time IoT cargo tracking — and finally validate with test deals from the founder's industry network.

Planned Step-by-Step Process

1

📤 Lot Creation & Verification

Seller uploads 50,000 MT fuel oil specification to GETH platform, attaches SGS quality certificate. AI system verifies seller's credentials, financial standing, and trading history.

Target: Counterparty risk scored in <10 minutes
2

🔍 AI Matching & Discovery

GETH's AI engine analyses buyer requirements and automatically matches with available lots. Intermediary receives notification with pre-calculated commission structure.

Target: Automated deal discovery — no cold calls
3

🔒 Escrow & Contract Lock

Buyer books the lot and deposits $19M into regulated escrow account. Smart contract locks all terms: price, quality specs, delivery timeline, intermediary commission.

Target: 0.5% platform fee + 0.1% escrow fee
4

🚢 Logistics Coordination

Seller confirms cargo readiness. Buyer selects preferred shipping route. Intermediary coordinates with carrier for vessel booking. IoT sensors activated for real-time tracking.

Target: Transparent logistics for all parties
5

📡 Real-Time Cargo Tracking

IoT devices monitor cargo temperature, humidity, and location throughout voyage. Satellite data streamed to GETH platform. Automated notifications at key milestones.

Target: Tamper-proof tracking data
6

✅ Quality Verification

Upon arrival, independent inspector verifies cargo quality. Buyer digitally signs acceptance. IoT data confirms no temperature excursions during transit.

Target: Instant confirmation — no 18-month disputes
7

💰 Automated Settlement

Smart contract releases $18.96M to seller (minus fees), $38k to intermediary. Settlement completes in under 2 hours instead of weeks.

Target: $28,500 GETH revenue (0.15% of deal)
8

📊 Reporting & Analytics

All participants access deal history via dashboard. Buyer receives comprehensive digital passport with ESG data. Complete audit trail for compliance.

Target: Complete audit trail for compliance
