What is GETH?

GETH (Global Energy & Commodity Trust Hub) is a next-generation platform designed to digitise and streamline over-the-counter (OTC) commodity trading. We address critical pain points in the $4.6 trillion market: 63% manual processes, widespread certificate fraud costing $50B+ annually, and dispute resolution delays exceeding 18 months.

Our phased approach starts with a concierge MVP — manually orchestrating deals with AI-assisted counterparty scoring and stablecoin payments — then evolves into a fully automated ecosystem with smart contracts, NFT-based quality certificates, IoT cargo tracking, and a dual-token economy ($GETH + $vGETH).

Our Solution

Phased Approach — Validate First, Automate Later

Phase 1 (Now)

Concierge MVP

Manual validation before automation. Prove demand with real deals using founder's industry network.

  • Manual deal matching with AI-assisted counterparty scoring
  • Stablecoin (USDC/USDT) or fiat payments via regulated escrow
  • API-based KYB/KYC verification (Sumsub, ComplyAdvantage)
  • 5–10 pilot deals with trusted traders
  • Partnership with existing IoT providers (no in-house hardware)
  • No tokens yet — focus on product-market fit
Phase 2 (12–24 mo)

Full Automation

Scale with technology after validating demand and unit economics.

  • Smart contracts for automated escrow and settlement
  • NFT-based quality certificates (SGS/Bureau Veritas integration)
  • $GETH token launch (exchange token with buyback mechanics)
  • Real-time IoT cargo tracking (temperature, GPS, tamper-proof)
  • $vGETH governance token (burn for certificates, DAO voting)
  • Automated arbitration based on DIFC-LCIA rules (14-day target)

Step-by-Step Process

1

📤 Lot Creation & Verification

Seller uploads 50,000 MT fuel oil specification to GETH platform, attaches SGS quality certificate. AI system instantly verifies seller's credentials, financial standing, and trading history.

✓ Counterparty risk scored in <10 minutes
2

🔍 AI Matching & Discovery

GETH's AI engine analyses buyer requirements and automatically matches with available lots. Intermediary receives notification about potential deal with pre-calculated commission structure.

✓ Automated deal discovery — no cold calls
3

🔒 Escrow & Contract Lock

Buyer books the lot and deposits $19M into regulated escrow account. Smart contract or legal agreement locks all terms: price, quality specs, delivery timeline, intermediary commission.

✓ 0.5% platform fee + 0.1% escrow fee applied
4

🚢 Logistics Coordination

Seller confirms cargo readiness. Buyer selects preferred shipping route. Intermediary coordinates with carrier for vessel booking. IoT sensors activated for real-time tracking.

✓ Transparent logistics — all parties see same data
5

📡 Real-Time Cargo Tracking

IoT devices monitor cargo temperature, humidity, and location throughout voyage. Satellite data streamed to GETH platform. Automated notifications at key milestones.

✓ Tamper-proof tracking data — no disputes
6

✅ Quality Verification

Upon arrival, independent inspector verifies cargo quality. Buyer digitally signs acceptance. IoT data confirms no temperature excursions during transit.

✓ Instant quality confirmation — no 18-month disputes
7

💰 Automated Settlement

Smart contract releases $18.96M to seller (minus fees), $38k to intermediary. Settlement completes in <2 hours instead of weeks.

✓ Total GETH revenue: $28,500 (0.15% of deal)
8

📊 Reporting & Analytics

All participants access deal history via dashboard. Buyer receives comprehensive digital passport with ESG data. Complete audit trail for compliance.

✓ Complete audit trail — ready for compliance
